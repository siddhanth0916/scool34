OUR JOURNEY

here is always a story behind every step taken towards improvement. The future of our nation lies in the hands of our children. To impart quality education is crucial from an early stage. After observing a dire need for value education and holistic learning methodology during the time, we established Sanskriti The School in 2009 under the Terala Educational Society. The board members are renowned personalities who laid the school foundation, with the intention to provide quality education and wholesome amenities to children from their early childhood. It is a platform for kids to enhance their social, secular, and ethical reasoning skills.
